Who & What We Are

Our Vision

To develop responsible, productive citizens through quality education based on core values (respect, compassion, integrity, commitment, responsibility), striving for excellence to participate in socio-economic growth.

Our Mission

Create exemplary students instilled with good Christian values, promoting a safe, caring environment with highly qualified educators and active parent involvement.

About Us

Registered in 2019 with the Department of Education. Glenmark Christian School is an independent private school providing quality education from Grade R-12 at affordable prices. Our management team consists of professional teachers with over 21 years of experience. We value all people irrespective of beliefs or origin, aligned to Christian standards.

Our Core Pillars

For a student to achieve maximum potential, three essential components work together.

01

Student

  • Be punctual and present
  • Dedicate to excellence in academics, sport, culture
  • Respect educators and fellow learners
  • Honor Code of Conduct & core values
02

Educators

  • Positive professional relationships
  • Create conducive environment
  • Develop talents through extra-curriculars
  • Implement Code of Conduct & core values
  • Refer learners for psycho-social support
03

Parents

  • Support learning & homework
  • Ensure attendance and safety
  • Attend meetings & support communication
  • Support governing body, principal, teachers
  • Engage in field trips & sports events
